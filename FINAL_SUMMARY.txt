## 📊 THE 6-STEP ANALYSIS PIPELINE

Step 1 → Data Preprocessing
         Clean, normalize, convert to INR

Step 2 → RFM Analysis
         Calculate Recency, Frequency, Monetary
         Identify 8 customer segments

         Reduce features to 2D
         Capture 75% of variance

Step 4 → Cluster Profiling
         Find optimal clusters (k=2-8)
         Profile each cluster

         Create 7 professional plots

         Generate executive summary
